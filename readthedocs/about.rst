******************************************
About Visplot
******************************************

Visplot is a visibility plot and observation scheduling tool designed for
astronomical telescopes. It helps observers plan and optimize an entire
observing night by automatically combining target visibility, constraints,
and priorities.

Visplot is intended for:

* Night-time observers planning complex programs

* Queue and service-mode scheduling

* Anyone who has ever tried to juggle airmass, time windows, and coffee

Key features include:

* Automatic, nearly-optimal scheduling

* Support for multiple telescopes and sites

* Interactive web interface and local installations

* Predefined targets and calibration standards
